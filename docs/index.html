Why Choose Form Filler?

Built for speed, accuracy, and privacy. The only form filler you'll ever need.

Smart Context Detection

Most fillers just look at `name='email'`. We look at the label, the placeholder, and the surrounding text. Our weighted scoring algorithm ensures the right data goes into the right field, every time.

Native Safari Support

Mac users, we heard you. Form Filler is built as a native Safari App Extension, ensuring smooth performance and full compatibility with the Apple ecosystem.

Localized Data

Testing a Vietnamese e-commerce site? Or a Japanese registration form? We generate culturally accurate names, addresses, and phone numbers for multiple locales.

Total Privacy

Your data is yours. Form Filler is open-source and operates entirely offline. No analytics, no tracking, no data sent to the cloud.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, it is 100% free and open-source. You can check the code on GitHub or contribute to the project.

Can I add my own custom data?

Absolutely. You can define custom fields using Regex or specific values in the Settings panel to match your specific testing needs.

Does it work on iframes?

Yes, Form Filler can detect and fill fields inside iframes, which is common in payment forms and embedded widgets.
